  1. Team newspublished at 19:37 British Summer Time
    19:37 BST

    Auckland City v Boca Juniors (20:00 BST)

    Auckland City have made four changes from the side that lost 6-0 to Benfica as they go in search of their first goal at the Club World Cup.

    Boca Juniors know goal difference could be a factor in whether they are to progress beyond this evening and they make three changes from the side that narrowly lost to Bayern Munich.

    Experienced striker Edinson Cavani is among those recalled to the starting line-up.

    Auckland City XI: Garrow; Murati, Mitchell, Gray, Boxall, Lagos; Yoo, Garriga, Ilich, Manickum; Bevan

    Boca Juniors XI: Marchesin; Advincula, Di Lollo, Pellegrino, Blanco; Battaglia, Palacios; Zeballos, Merentiel, Velasco; Cavani

  2. Team news - Otamendi and Di Maria in for Benfica, Kane among Bayern subspublished at 19:35 British Summer Time
    19:35 BST

    Benfica v Bayern Munich (20:00 BST)

    Benfica need a point to secure a top-two spot and their side includes ex-Manchester City defender Nicolas Otamendi and former Manchester United winger Angel Di Maria.

    Renato Sanches, who had a spell at Swansea City, is one of three players to come into the starting line-up from the side that thrashed Auckland City 6-0.

    Benfica XI: Trubin, Dahl, Otamendi, Silva, Aursnes, Schjelderup, Barreiro, Sanches, Prestianni, Di Maria, Pavlidis.

    Bayern Munich have already secured their spot in the knockout stages and Vincent Kompany makes seven changes to the 11 that began the 2-1 win over Boca Juniors.

    England's record goalscorer Harry Kane is one of those to be given a rest, with ex-Crystal Palace winger Michael Olise also among the substitutes, while former Manchester City man Leroy Sane and club legend Thomas Muller are both starting.

    Bayern Munich XI: Neuer, Guerreiro, Stanisic, Boey, Upamecano, Palhinha, Bischof, Pavlovic, Sane, Gnabry, Muller.
